About Us

Currently more than 55 million people have dementia worldwide, over 60% of whom live in low and middle income countries. Every year, there are nearly 10 million new cases.
Dementia results from a variety of diseasr and injuries that affect the brain. Alzheimer's disease is the most common form of dementia and may contribute 60-70% of cases.

Living with Dementia?

We're proud to have worked in partnership with Alzheimer's Society. Together we've built stronger, dementia-friendly communities and are making FIN BANK a dementia-friendly business We want our customers to be able to bank in the way that makes them most comfortable. That's why many of our staff are Dementia Friends, meaning they're trained to support those impacted by dementia.
